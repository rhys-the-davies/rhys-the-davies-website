About

rhys-the-davies is a Product Manager at Canonical where he primarily focuses the success of IoT/devices and community related products. He has been deeply interested in technology, open engineering and story telling for as long as he can remember. His role as a Product Manager for Canonical, the company that publishes Ubuntu, was his first long term role outside of University.

Before joining Canonical, Rhys graduated from the University of Exeter with a 1st class honours masters degree in mechanical engineering with international study in the University of Waterloo where he studied mechatronics. Before that Rhys worked as an R&D engineer for CyberHawk innovations in Edinburgh, a reserch intern at the Masdar Institute in Abu Dhabi and a circus specialist in Pennsylvania.

Originally from Wales, Rhys grew up by the sea supported by books and a small Aikido club where he discovered community, confidence and kindness. He believes you do not need to be an asshole to be successful and that he will one day retire to open a pub like the Eolian from the King Killer Chronicle.
